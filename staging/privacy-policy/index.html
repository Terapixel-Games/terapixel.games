Terapixel Games is committed to responsible data handling. This page explains what information may be collected when you use our website and browser games.

This site may use cookies and similar technologies for performance measurement and to support advertising, including Google AdSense. Third-party providers may use cookies to serve and measure ads.

Some game content is delivered through third-party platforms. Those services may process data under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this policy as products evolve. Updates are posted here with a revised effective date.
