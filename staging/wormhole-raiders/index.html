About Wormhole Raiders

Wormhole Raiders is a browser action game built around dodging pressure, quick reactions, and tactical movement.

The game rewards players who can stay calm while conditions change quickly. With each attempt, you can refine route choices and improve your survivability.

How To Play

Keep moving, read threats early, and take safe windows when they appear to survive longer and improve your performance.

  • Action-focused gameplay that rewards reflexes
  • Short rounds with strong replayability
  • Runs that encourage constant improvement
